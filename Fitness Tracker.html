Jump Rope

Improves cardiovascular endurance and coordination.

  • Hold the rope handles and keep your elbows close.
  • Jump lightly as the rope swings under your feet.
  • Maintain a steady rhythm.

Deadlifts

Builds strength in the lower back, hamstrings, and glutes.

  • Stand with feet hip-width apart.
  • Grip the barbell and lift by extending your hips and knees.
  • Lower the barbell back down in a controlled motion.
Watch Tutorial

Pull-ups

Great for building upper body and grip strength.

  • Grab the pull-up bar with an overhand grip.
  • Pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar.
  • Lower yourself back down with control.
